About Us

Located beside Queenborough Creek, The Little Blue Hut is a small independent seaside seafood hut serving freshly prepared shellfish and hot food in a relaxed coastal setting.

We carefully source our seafood for freshness and flavour and keep things simple — proper honest food done well. Our menu includes dressed crab, prawns, seafood pots and classic seaside favourites.

Alongside seafood we serve hot drinks, cold refreshments, alcoholic beverages, baguettes and freshly baked jacket potatoes — perfect to enjoy by the water after a walk around the harbour.
