About Us

Located beside Queenborough Creek, The Little Blue Hut is a small independent seaside seafood hut serving freshly prepared shellfish and hot food in a relaxed coastal setting.

We carefully source our seafood for freshness and flavour and keep things simple — proper honest food done well.
