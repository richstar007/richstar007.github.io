About Us

Located beside Queenborough Creek, The Little Blue Hut is a small independent seaside seafood hut serving freshly prepared shellfish and hot food in a relaxed coastal setting.

We carefully source our seafood for freshness and flavour and serve simple food done properly. Alongside seafood we offer hot drinks, cold refreshments, baguettes and freshly baked jacket potatoes.

We also stock a selection of alcoholic drinks including chilled beers and ciders — perfect to enjoy by the water after a walk along the harbour.
