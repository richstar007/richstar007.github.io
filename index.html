About Us

At The Little Blue Hut, we are committed to bringing you the highest-quality, responsibly sourced seafood that not only tastes exceptional but also looks stunning. All of our seafood is thoughtfully hand-selected and carefully tasted before being served.

We also sell hot & cold drinks, a selection of alcoholic beverages and delicious baguettes and freshly baked jacket potatoes.

We also offer free delivery to Sheppey and Sittingbourne (subject to a min £20 spend).
